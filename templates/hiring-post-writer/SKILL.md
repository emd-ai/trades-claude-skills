## What you need to tell me

Pre-configured:
- Business name, phone, email, trade, service area
- Years in business, team size, benefits
- Referral bonus ({{REFERRAL_BONUS}})

You still need to tell me:
- **Role** (e.g. "HVAC service technician", "journeyman plumber")
- **Experience level** (licensed, mid-level, apprentice)
- **Pay range** (hourly or annual)
- **Schedule** (Mon–Fri, on-call, seasonal?)
- **What a typical day looks like** (optional but strongly recommended)
- **Growth path** (optional — where this role leads in 2–3 years)
